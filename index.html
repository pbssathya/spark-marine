Company Profile

Spark Marine Company Limited, head office in Dammam, Saudi Arabia, has over 20 years of proven experience in Marine Electronics, Communication, Navigation, Automation, and Environmental Equipment.

Our diverse engineering team brings deep technical expertise in repairing and maintaining onboard equipment, providing round-the-clock service for vessels at Saudi Arabia and also at Bahrain and overseas ports. The professional excellence and dedication of our certified engineers form the cornerstone of Spark Marine’s reputation in both domestic and international markets.

Our Strength

Spark Marine Company Limited leads in Integration Solutions and quality service across the Middle East, particularly in Saudi Arabia and the GCC Coastal Region. We are proud to be among the few companies fully equipped with the necessary facilities, backed by a dedicated team of engineers and designers. Our response time is among the fastest in the industry—within 30 minutes—demonstrating our commitment to valuing our customers’ time.
