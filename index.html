Company Profile

Spark Marine Company Limited, headquartered in Singapore and based in Dammam, Saudi Arabia, has over 20 years of proven experience in Marine Electronics, Communication, Navigation, Automation, and Environmental Equipment. With additional offices in Bahrain, India, Singapore, and Indonesia, the company operates under IMO and SOLAS regulations, ensuring compliance and reliability in every service.

Our diverse engineering team brings deep technical expertise in repairing and maintaining onboard equipment, providing round-the-clock service for vessels at Bahrain and overseas ports. The professional excellence and dedication of our certified engineers form the cornerstone of Spark Marine’s reputation in both domestic and international markets.

Our Strength

Spark Marine Company Limited leads in integration solutions and quality service across the Middle East, particularly in Saudi Arabia and the GCC coastal region. We are proud to be among the few companies fully equipped with the necessary facilities, backed by a dedicated team of engineers and designers. Our response time is among the fastest in the industry—within 30 minutes—demonstrating our commitment to valuing our customers’ time.

We also extend our expertise globally, offering services at ports across Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, Dubai, Singapore, and Indonesia. From installation to commissioning and turnkey project management, Spark Marine goes the extra mile to fulfill every customer commitment with precision and reliability.

Quality

We deliver high-quality services for Marine Electronics, Electrical, Communication, Environmental, and Navigational Systems, as well as Automation and Instrumentation. Spark Marine never compromises on quality — achieving high levels of customer satisfaction and building long-term relationships. Our engineers are equipped with the latest communication technology, ensuring 24×7 global connectivity.

Vision

To make everyone comfortable with the latest and fast-changing technologies — becoming one of the most trusted, grounded, and solution-oriented marine integrators in the world.
